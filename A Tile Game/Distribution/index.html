Game Description:

Collect coins, shoot enemies to kill them, and go for the exit to win the level!


Game Modifications:

  1. The game has a shoot functionality.
  2. The player cannot double jump.
  3. The player can climb ladders.
  4. There are two types of enemies with different health levels.
